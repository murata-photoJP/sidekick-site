Why Sidekick Lab

What sets it apart

🛠

Built from personal frustration

These tools weren't built by imagining what users might need. They were built to automate the repetitive work Murata did every night in the field. Only what was genuinely necessary made it in.

🔒

No generative AI. No cloud upload.

Removal is done by replacing original pixels — no AI generation, no image alteration, no data sent to the cloud. Everything runs inside your own Photoshop installation.

🏆

20 years of field experience in the defaults

1X Award ×5, 6 published books, CP+ conference speaker. The processing judgments Murata refined over two decades in the field are baked into the script parameters. These aren't presets — they have reasons.

📷

The tools are just the beginning

Workshops go beyond "how to use it" — they cover the why behind every processing decision. Using the tools while learning the thinking raises the level of your own judgment.
